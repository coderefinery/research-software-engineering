Choose your own adventure

Each episode has exercises or something to think about. There are too many exercises to complete in 1.5 hours. Please start with the ones that are most relevant to you and your work and try to complete one or two topics. If you have time, you can continue with the remaining exercises. If you have the possibility to work in pairs, please do so.
